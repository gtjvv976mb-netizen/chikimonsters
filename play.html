📖 Game Mechanics & Economy ✕

🌍 What is Chiki Monsters?

An idle voxel world tied to the $CHIKI token. Every wallet holding at least 500,000 $CHIKI automatically receives a Chiki — an autonomous creature that lives in the block world and earns real SOL for its holder by completing tasks. You don't control it directly; you watch, upgrade, and collect.

🕹 How to play

  • Connect your wallet (top right). If you hold ≥500k $CHIKI, your Chiki appears with a ▼ YOU marker.
  • Explore in 3D: drag to orbit the camera, scroll to zoom, WASD to pan, click the minimap to jump. Press 🎯 Track to lock the camera onto your Chiki.
  • Watch it work: it wanders, picks tasks, travels to the right season zone, and gets paid into your wallet automatically.
  • Level it up from the My Chiki panel to make it stronger.

🎲 Tasks & rewards

12 tasks ranked from highest to lowest chance — the rarer the task, the bigger the payout, each paying a % of the live creator-fee pool (see 📋 Task Table). The pool refills continuously from pump.fun creator fees on $CHIKI trades. Every task is timed (4s → 20s); travelling Chikis beam a light pillar at their destination, working Chikis show their task icon and a progress bar until the SOL drops.

⚡ Stamina & sleep

Every task costs stamina (8⚡ → 34⚡). At zero, your Chiki sleeps and earns nothing. Higher level = bigger stamina tank (92⚡ at Lv1 → 680⚡ at Lv50) and shorter sleep (64s → 10s).

🌟 Levels & evolution (max Lv 50)

  • Buy levels with $CHIKI or SOL (SOL path costs ~15% more).
  • Evolution at Lv 15 and Lv 35 — bigger voxel forms with wings, then a crown.
  • Each stage adds +35% odds on Epic+ tasks; every level adds +1.5% more.

📈 XP, events & achievements

Chikis also earn XP from every task and level up for free over time — paying just gets there faster. Watch the sky for random ⛈ Treasure Storms (all payouts ×2) and ✨ Shiny Hours (rare odds ×2), collect 🏅 achievements, give your Chiki a custom name (My Chiki panel), and whales holding 1M+ $CHIKI get a second Chiki earning for them. Explore the new 🌋 volcano island off the east coast and spot the floating sky islands — the valley now has rain, fireflies at night, chimney smoke, glowing windows and circling birds.

💰 The economy

  • Inflow: creator fees from every $CHIKI trade stream into the reward pool.
  • Outflow: task payouts (a % of pool, so it can never fully drain).
  • Every upgrade & purchase splits 20 / 45 / 35: 🔥 20% burned, 💰 45% into the reward pool, 🏦 35% to the team wallet.
  • Level costs grow 9%/level (Lv1→2 ≈ 2,725 $CHIKI; Lv49→50 ≈ 170k). Maxing one Chiki burns ≈ 509k $CHIKI.
  • $CHIKI payments are blocked if they'd drop you below the 500k threshold.

🧊 Blockbench Model Studio ✕

Design your Chikis in Blockbench (free at blockbench.net): open a .bbmodel from the Chiki starter pack, move/add cubes and repaint the palette texture, save, then load the file here — that species transforms in-game instantly and is remembered by your browser.

Rules: keep cubes axis-aligned (no rotations), face the model toward +Z (south), feet at Y = 0. Each cube's colour is sampled from its texture face.

.glb also works (Blender, Meshy, Tripo…): models are auto-scaled, centred and grounded — face them toward +Z and keep them low-poly (<10k tris).
