🎮 How to Play

  • Connect your wallet in-game (top right). Hold ≥500k $CHIKI and your Chiki appears with a YOU marker.
  • Open My Chiki for your personal stats: level, XP, stamina, earnings and achievements.
  • Name your Chiki — your custom name is saved to your wallet.
  • Press Track to follow your Chiki, or roam Chikoria freely (drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, WASD to move).
  • Level up with $CHIKI or SOL — bigger stamina, shorter sleep, and evolutions that boost rare-task odds.

Tasks & rewards

12 timed tasks ranked from common to legendary — the rarer the task, the bigger the SOL payout (and the longer it takes, from 1 minute up to 30). Chikis rest between bursts of work so the reward pool can refill.
